BSMRSTU CSE Fest 2022

Date : Coming Soon

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman Science and Technology University will host 'BSMRSTU CSE FEST 2022, ' one of Bangladesh's largest tech carnivals, in the coming days at the BSMRSTU Campus in Gopalganj. We do have several significant sections with a large reward pool this year. This mega event will be highlighted by segments such as Intra-University Programming Contest, Project Showcasing, Idea Contest, Debating, Technical Writing Contest, Seminars and Exhibition, Gamming, Math Contest, and so on. All students from diverse departments are invited to attend this important event.

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established with the goal of providing quality education in the field of computer science. The department has cutting-edge teaching, learning, and research facilities. The department provides a diverse range of research opportunities and study programs at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels. With rapidly changing technology and a constant need for innovation, the department has produced quality professionals who now hold important positions in the IT industry both at home and abroad.

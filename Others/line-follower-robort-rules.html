Line Follower Robot Rules

General Rules:
  1. The participants of this competition must be registered under a team. A team must have a team name and a team leader. A team can have maximum 3 members.
  2. The teams have to make an autonomous line follower robot that follow black lines on the white surface under various conditions.
  3. Number of checkpoints and the time limit will be declared on the competition day.
  4. The winning team is declared based on the points.
  5. Each team has 3 restarts.
  6. Only 1 member from the team is allowed in the arena during the main run.
Disqualify Rules:
The following will lead a team to be disqualified:
  1. Evidence of disrespect to other teams and competitors.
  2. Evidence of disrespect to competition judges.
TBA: Arena Specification and track, Robot Specifications, Point Criteria etc.
Gameplay Rules:
  1. The teams must submit their robots for the ratification before the competition. During one’s turn, he can receive the robot from the organizers.
  2. Each team will get 1 minute of calibration time before the main run. In case any team takes more than 1 minute of calibration time, the extra time will be subtracted from the main run time.
  3. At the start of the main run, the timer is reset and with the instruction of the host the timer starts. The operating member of the team then starts the robot from the Start zone.
  4. During the run time, picking up the robot or even touching it will cost a restart for the team.
  5. During any turn, if the total chassis of the robot gets out of the line, it will cost a restart.
  6. In case of a restart, the operating member of the team can pick up the robot only after the declaration of a restart by the host. Then s/he will have to put the robot before the checkpoint marking.
  7. During the run time, if the operating member wants to take a restart at any instant, s/he can do so by informing the host.
  8. If the robot stops at the Finish zone, the team will get bonus points.
NOTE These are the initial common rules. We will announce the detailed rules along with registration links on our Facebook event page soon. Stay tuned!
